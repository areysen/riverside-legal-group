About Riverside Legal Group

Your trusted legal advocates in Portland for over 15 years

Our Story

Founded in 2008 by Sarah Mitchell, Riverside Legal Group began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional legal representation with a personal touch. What started as a small practice in downtown Portland has grown into one of the region's most respected law firms.

Our founding principle was to treat every client like family, understanding that legal matters often come during life's most challenging moments. This philosophy has guided our growth and continues to define how we serve our community today.

Over the past 15+ years, we've helped thousands of individuals and businesses navigate complex legal challenges, securing millions in compensation for injury victims, guiding businesses through critical decisions, and helping families protect their futures through comprehensive estate planning.
