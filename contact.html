Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about contacting and working with our firm

How much does a consultation cost?

All initial consultations are completely free. We believe everyone should have access to quality legal advice without financial barriers.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

We typically schedule consultations within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, we can often accommodate same-day appointments.

What should I bring to my consultation?

Bring any relevant documents, police reports, medical records, or correspondence related to your case. If you don't have documents yet, that's okay too.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer phone and video consultations for your convenience. We can accommodate both in-person and remote meetings.

What if I can't afford an attorney?

We offer flexible payment plans and work on contingency fees for personal injury cases. We also provide pro bono services for qualifying cases.

How do I know if I have a case?

Every situation is unique. The best way to determine if you have a case is to schedule a free consultation where we can evaluate your specific circumstances.
