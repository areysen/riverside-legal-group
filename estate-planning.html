Our Estate Planning Process

A step-by-step approach to securing your legacy

1

Initial Consultation

We meet to discuss your family situation, assets, goals, and concerns. This helps us understand your unique needs and recommend the most appropriate estate planning strategies.

2

Strategy Development

Based on our consultation, we develop a comprehensive estate planning strategy that addresses your specific circumstances and achieves your goals.

3

Document Preparation

We draft all necessary documents, ensuring they comply with Oregon law and accurately reflect your wishes. You'll have the opportunity to review and revise as needed.

4

Document Execution

We guide you through the proper execution of your estate planning documents, ensuring they are legally valid and enforceable.

5

Ongoing Support

We provide ongoing support and guidance, helping you update your plan as your circumstances change and ensuring your estate plan remains current and effective.
