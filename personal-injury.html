What to Do After an Accident

Taking the right steps immediately after an accident can significantly impact your ability to recover compensation. Here's what you should do:

1

Seek Medical Attention

Your health is the priority. Get medical treatment immediately, even if you don't think you're seriously injured. Some injuries may not be apparent right away.

2

Document Everything

Take photos of the accident scene, your injuries, and any property damage. Get contact information from witnesses and exchange information with other parties.

3

Report the Accident

File a police report for car accidents or report the incident to the property owner for slip and fall cases. Get a copy of any reports.

4

Don't Give Statements

Avoid giving recorded statements to insurance companies without consulting an attorney. They may try to use your words against you.

5

Contact an Attorney

Call us as soon as possible. The sooner we get involved, the better we can protect your rights and build a strong case.
